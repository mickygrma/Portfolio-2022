Hi there

I am illustrator and design artist based in Ethiopia with 2+ year experience that is eager to work on any artistic project. i have a solid background in terms of design software and technical and communicational aspects of each creative process. I can adapt to many different styles, according to what the project requires.I specially enjoy projects which involve a graphic narrative and character design.

Ability to develop the process from sketching up to delivering final rendered character illustrations. Able to offer a variety of styles, from vector artwork and cartoony style to realistic and highly detailed digital renders, and easily adaptable to your needs regarding the functionality and application of the character.

I'm experienced in Infographic design, Magazine design & layout, Logo design and designing marketing materials for print, web and social media.
