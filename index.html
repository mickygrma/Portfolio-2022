Ola!

I'm an illustrator and design artist based in Ethiopia with over 2 years of experience in creating awesome artwork. I'm always excited to take on new artistic projects and bring my creativity to the table. With a solid background in design software and technical and communicational aspects of the creative process, I can adapt to many different styles according to what the project requires!

I have a passion for projects that involve a graphic narrative and character design. From sketching to delivering final rendered character illustrations, I'm able to offer a variety of styles such as vector artwork, cartoony style, realistic and highly detailed digital renders. I'm easily adaptable to your needs regarding the functionality and application of the character.

My expertise also extends to infographic design, magazine design & layout, logo design, and designing marketing materials for print, web, and social media. So, if you're looking for a fun and creative artist to bring your project to life, I'm your guy!
